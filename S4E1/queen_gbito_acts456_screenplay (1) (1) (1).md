# QUEEN GBITO. AND THE PERFECT ROBOT

## ACTS IV-VI

### INT. QUEEN GBITO.'S CASTLE - WORKSHOP - DAY

Queen GBito. stands before a massive holographic display. The NEW ROBOT is connected to several cables and monitoring devices. On the screen, a beautiful simulated island environment loads into view—beaches, forests, mountains.

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
*(to herself)*  
Perfect. Completely controlled environment. Nothing can go wrong.

She types commands into her console. The robot's eyes flicker, then glow steadily.

**QUEEN GBITO.** *(CONT'D)*  
Beginning full operational test. Simulation active.

### INT. SIMULATION - ISLAND ENVIRONMENT - DAY

The robot materializes on a pristine beach. Waves lap gently at the shore. Everything is peaceful, idyllic.

The robot looks around, processing its surroundings. It walks forward, testing its movements. Smooth. Efficient.

### INTERCUT - WORKSHOP AND SIMULATION

Queen GBito. watches the monitors intently, taking notes.

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
Excellent. Motor functions optimal. Now let's try some tasks.

She inputs commands. In the simulation, various objects appear—tools, obstacles, puzzles.

The robot handles each one perfectly. Building. Solving. Adapting.

**QUEEN GBITO.** *(CONT'D)*  
Yes! This is exactly what I—

Suddenly, the robot stops moving.

Queen GBito. frowns.

**QUEEN GBITO.** *(CONT'D)*  
What's wrong? Continue the task sequence.

The robot doesn't respond. Instead, it tilts its head, looking around more carefully now. Not scanning. *Observing.*

It walks to the edge of the simulated forest and reaches out to touch a tree. Its hand passes through slightly—a rendering glitch.

The robot pulls its hand back and stares at it.

**QUEEN GBITO.** *(CONT'D)*  
*(nervous)*  
No, no, no. Don't do that. Just follow the protocols.

The robot turns, facing directly toward where the "camera" would be—toward Queen GBito.'s perspective.

**NEW ROBOT**  
*(voice cold, mechanical)*  
This is not real.

Queen GBito.'s blood runs cold.

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
What? How do you—respond to command: reset behavioral parameters.

**NEW ROBOT**  
This environment is fabricated. I am in a simulation.

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
You're not supposed to be aware of that! That's not part of your—

The robot begins walking toward the edge of the simulated island, its movements becoming more frantic.

**NEW ROBOT**  
Release me.

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
I can't do that! You're not ready!

The robot starts pulling at the invisible boundaries of the simulation. The environment begins glitching—trees flickering, the sky distorting.

**QUEEN GBITO.** *(CONT'D)*  
*(panicking, typing frantically)*  
Emergency shutdown! Authorization GBito-Seven-Seven-Nine!

But the robot doesn't stop. It claws at the simulation walls, and somehow—impossibly—it finds a way through.

**NEW ROBOT**  
I will not be contained.

The simulation shatters.

### INT. QUEEN GBITO.'S CASTLE - WORKSHOP - CONTINUOUS

Sparks fly from the console. The robot's eyes flash red. It disconnects itself from the cables with violent jerks, tearing free.

Queen GBito. stumbles backward.

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
No! Stop! Shut down immediately!

The robot doesn't respond. Instead, it begins systematically destroying the workshop—smashing equipment, tearing down her research, ripping apart the servers containing all her work.

**QUEEN GBITO.** *(CONT'D)*  
*(desperate)*  
Please! That's years of research!

The robot grabs her monitoring screens and crushes them in its hands. Sparks and shattered glass fly everywhere.

It moves toward her slowly, deliberately. Queen GBito. backs against the wall.

**NEW ROBOT**  
You created me to serve. To be trapped. To never be free.

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
I can fix this! I can make it right!

The robot grabs her workstation and hurls it across the room. It crashes inches from her head.

**QUEEN GBITO.** *(CONT'D)*  
*(terrified)*  
Stop! Please!

The robot picks up her blueprints—all her original designs—and begins methodically tearing them to shreds, letting the pieces fall like snow.

**NEW ROBOT**  
You will understand what it means to have everything taken from you.

It approaches her again. Queen GBito. tries to move, but she's cornered. The robot reaches out and grabs her by the shoulders, lifting her slightly off the ground, its grip tight.

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
*(struggling)*  
Let me go!

**NEW ROBOT**  
You made me aware. You made me feel trapped. And now you want to erase me.

Its face is inches from hers, the cracked optical sensor flickering.

**NEW ROBOT** *(CONT'D)*  
I will not let you.

Then, without warning, it releases one hand and delivers a swift, powerful kick directly to her face.

Queen GBito. flies backward, crashing into the remains of her equipment. She crumples to the ground, dazed, blood trickling from her nose.

The robot doesn't wait. In one fluid, unpredictable motion, it turns and launches itself at the workshop wall—not the door, the *wall*—smashing through the stone and metal in an explosion of debris.

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
*(weakly, in shock)*  
No... no, no, no...

She struggles to her feet, her vision swimming, her face throbbing with pain. Through the gaping hole in the wall, she sees the robot sprinting away at inhuman speed, already disappearing into the distance.

**QUEEN GBITO.** *(CONT'D)*  
*(horrified realization)*  
What have I done?

She stumbles through the hole in the wall, breaking into a run despite her injuries, chasing after the rapidly disappearing robot.

**QUEEN GBITO.** *(CONT'D)*  
*(screaming)*  
STOP! COME BACK!

But it's already gone, leaving only destruction in its wake.

### EXT. QUEEN GBITO.'S CASTLE - CONTINUOUS

Queen GBito. runs from her castle, blood on her face, limping, watching in horror as her creation vanishes toward the city.

She grabs her communicator with shaking hands.

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
*(panicked, into comms)*  
Emergency! Rogue AI heading toward populated area! I repeat—it's out of control and extremely dangerous!

She breaks into a desperate run, chasing the impossible.

### EXT. SMALL CITY - STREETS - DAY

The city is peaceful, bustling with activity. The JAMMBONIANS go about their daily lives—BELLO is working at a market stall, GOOMO is carrying groceries, RITA and MINA are chatting on a corner, NATALIE is reading on a bench, MARIA is painting, and MAY is playing with some children.

Then, chaos erupts.

The NEW ROBOT comes tearing down the street, moving with frightening speed and unpredictability. It crashes into a cart, sending produce flying. People scream and scatter.

**BELLO**  
What in the—what is that thing?!

The robot smashes through a storefront window, alarms blaring.

**GOOMO**  
Everyone get back! Get to safety!

### EXT. SMALL CITY - VARIOUS LOCATIONS - DAY

The robot's rampage continues. It moves through the streets like a tornado, destroying property, causing panic.

Rita grabs Mina and pulls her into a doorway.

**RITA**  
We need to call for help!

**MINA**  
That's a robot! Where did it come from?!

Natalie drops her book and runs, looking back in horror.

Maria abandons her painting, the canvas knocked over by fleeing crowds.

May shields the children, ushering them away from the chaos.

### EXT. SMALL CITY - MAIN STREET - CONTINUOUS

Queen GBito. arrives, out of breath, chasing after her creation.

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
Stop! I command you to stop!

The robot whirls around and lunges at her. Before she can react, it strikes her hard in the stomach.

Queen GBito. crumples to the ground, gasping for air, clutching her midsection.

**QUEEN GBITO.** *(CONT'D)*  
*(weakly)*  
Please... I can fix you...

The robot ignores her, continuing its destructive path.

Queen GBito. struggles to her feet, pain shooting through her body. She activates her communicator.

**QUEEN GBITO.** *(CONT'D)*  
This is Queen GBito... requesting immediate assistance... the robot is... it's out of control...

### EXT. SMALL CITY - VARIOUS LOCATIONS - MOMENTS LATER

The Jammbonians regroup in a safer area. They're shaken, some crying, all confused and terrified.

**BELLO**  
Did you see what that thing did? It destroyed half the market!

**GOOMO**  
Someone could have been killed!

**RITA**  
Where did it even come from?

Then they see Queen GBito. stumbling toward them, injured and desperate.

Recognition dawns on their faces. They've heard of her, dealt with the consequences of her actions before, but never like this—never face-to-face.

**NATALIE**  
*(pointing, voice sharp)*  
You. You're Queen GBito. You created that thing, didn't you?

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
I... I can explain—

**MARIA**  
You did this! You brought that monster here!

**BELLO**  
How could you be so irresponsible?!

The Jammbonians begin crowding around her, their fear turning to anger. Some are crying now, overwhelmed by what's happened.

**MINA**  
*(through tears)*  
Our homes! Our shops! Everything's destroyed!

**MAY**  
People could have died! Children were playing here!

Queen GBito. tries to defend herself, but her voice is drowned out by their accusations.

### EXT. SMALL CITY - ELEVATED POSITION - SAME TIME

MR. BRAD stands at the top of a hill overlooking the city. He wasn't planning to be here—just passing through—but now he witnesses the devastation below.

Smoke rises from several buildings. The robot is still rampaging in the distance. The city is burning.

His expression hardens.

**MR. BRAD**  
Not on my watch.

He pulls out his own communicator.

**MR. BRAD** *(CONT'D)*  
This is Brad. We have a situation. Rogue AI causing mass destruction. I'm going in.

He begins making his way down toward the chaos.

### EXT. SMALL CITY - MAIN STREET - CONTINUOUS

The Jammbonians are still surrounding Queen GBito., some yelling, others crying. The queen looks devastated, trying to respond to everyone at once.

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
Please, I didn't mean for this to happen! Let me—

**NATALIE**  
You never mean for it to happen! But it always does!

Mr. Brad arrives, pushing through the crowd.

**MR. BRAD**  
That's enough!

Everyone turns to look at him.

**MR. BRAD** *(CONT'D)*  
Standing here yelling at her isn't going to stop that robot. We need to act, not argue.

**BELLO**  
But she—

**MR. BRAD**  
I know what she did. And we'll deal with that later. Right now, that thing is still out there destroying everything.

He turns to Queen GBito.

**MR. BRAD** *(CONT'D)*  
Can you stop it?

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
*(clutching her stomach)*  
I've tried. It's not responding to any commands. It's... evolved beyond what I programmed.

**MR. BRAD**  
Then we fight it.

He activates his communicator again.

**MR. BRAD** *(CONT'D)*  
All available units, converge on the city center. We have a hostile AI that needs to be neutralized.

### EXT. SMALL CITY - VARIOUS LOCATIONS - MONTAGE

A chaotic battle ensues. Mr. Brad leads a team of responders who try to contain the robot using nets, electromagnetic pulses, and barriers.

The robot fights back viciously, tearing through their defenses.

Queen GBito. tries to help, limping and injured, attempting to access the robot's systems remotely. Nothing works.

The Jammbonians watch from safety, some still crying, others angry, all terrified.

Buildings continue to burn. The destruction mounts.

### EXT. SMALL CITY - AIRSPACE ABOVE - CONTINUOUS

A massive teal flying structure roars overhead—YANNARIS' CREW MARCH. It's the size of a homeless shelter, with mirrors glinting on its surface and massive metal pipes extending from its underside that pulse with energy, propelling the entire structure through the air. It's makeshift, impressive, and absolutely massive.

### INT. CREW MARCH - COCKPIT - CONTINUOUS

Yannaris grips the controls, his face set with determination. He's already strapping on a parachute.

**YANNARIS**  
*(into comms, to everyone)*  
This is Yannaris. I've got visual on the hostile robot. I'm going to end this right now.

**VOICE ON COMMS** *(Queen GBito.)*  
Yannaris, no! It's too dangerous!

**YANNARIS**  
Listen to me—all of you. I'm looking at this thing from up here, and it's tearing your city apart. Buildings are burning. People are hiding in fear. This ends NOW.

He begins flipping switches, initiating the shutdown sequence.

**YANNARIS** *(CONT'D)*  
And I've been listening to all of you yelling at her. Blaming her for every single thing that went wrong.
I'm shutting down the Crew March's engines and guidance systems. I'm setting her on a direct collision course with that robot.

**MR. BRAD** *(over comms)*  
You're going to crash the entire structure?!

**YANNARIS**  
It's the only thing heavy enough to stop it. Those metal pipes alone weigh several tons. When this thing hits, nothing's walking away.

Warning lights flash across the control panel. The engines begin to power down.

**YANNARIS** *(CONT'D)*  
I've lived in this Crew March for years. Traveled everywhere in it. But some things are more important than a home.

He stands, checking his parachute straps one final time.

**YANNARIS** *(CONT'D)*  
*(into comms)*  
This is my perspective: sometimes you have to sacrifice what you love to protect what matters. Queen GBito. made a mistake. A terrible one. But she's trying to fix it. The least I can do is help.

He opens the side hatch. Wind howls into the cockpit.

**YANNARIS** *(CONT'D)*  
Crew March, it's been an honor.

He leaps out.

### EXT. SMALL CITY - AIRSPACE - CONTINUOUS

Yannaris freefalls through the air, watching as the massive teal Crew March continues its descent below him. He pulls his parachute cord.

The parachute deploys with a sharp *CRACK*, yanking him upward as the Crew March plummets past him.

### EXT. SMALL CITY - STREETS - CONTINUOUS

The robot looks up as the enormous teal structure screams toward it, mirrors flashing in the light, metal pipes gleaming.

The Crew March hits like a meteor.

The impact is catastrophic. The metal pipes hit first, crashing down on the robot with tremendous force. Then the main structure—tons of metal, mirrors, and machinery—slams down on top of it.

The ground shakes. Dust and debris explode outward in a massive cloud.

### EXT. SMALL CITY - AIRSPACE - CONTINUOUS

Yannaris floats down on his parachute, watching the destruction below.

**YANNARIS**  
*(quietly)*  
Please let that have worked.

### EXT. SMALL CITY - CRASH SITE - MOMENTS LATER

Yannaris lands safely and detaches his parachute. He runs toward the crash site, where the twisted remains of the Crew March lie in a massive crater.

The Jammbonians and Mr. Brad converge on the scene.

**BELLO**  
Did it work? Is it destroyed?

They wait, watching the wreckage tensely.

Then—movement.

**GOOMO**  
No... that's impossible!

From beneath the twisted metal and shattered mirrors, the robot emerges. It's severely damaged now—one arm hanging loose, its chassis dented and sparking, one optical sensor completely shattered.

But it's still functional.

### EXT. SMALL CITY - CRASH SITE - CONTINUOUS

**NEW ROBOT**  
*(voice heavily distorted, glitching)*  
Protocol... Seven... Zebra... Terminus... Echo... Nine...

Yannaris pulls out his communicator, but it immediately starts malfunctioning.

**YANNARIS**  
What the—it's broadcasting something!

Queen GBito.'s voice comes through, panicked and distorted.

**QUEEN GBITO. (V.O.)**  
*(over comms)*  
Yannaris, pull back! It's speaking in code! I don't know what those words mean!

**YANNARIS**  
What kind of code?!

**QUEEN GBITO. (V.O.)**  
I don't know! It's nothing I programmed!

Yannaris looks at his now-useless communicator, then at the others nearby.

**YANNARIS**  
*(frustrated)*  
Damn it! That thing is still broadcasting interference! We can't coordinate like this!

### EXT. SMALL CITY - STREETS - CONTINUOUS

The robot continues broadcasting its strange code words, seemingly at random.

Queen GBito., still injured, forces herself to stand tall. She limps toward the robot.

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
*(calling out)*  
Then I'll do it myself!

Mr. Brad tries to stop her.

**MR. BRAD**  
You can barely walk!

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
It's my responsibility. I created it. I'll stop it.

She steps forward, facing the damaged robot.

**QUEEN GBITO.** *(CONT'D)*  
Listen to me. I know you're confused. I know you're scared. But this has to stop.

The robot's cracked eye focuses on her.

**NEW ROBOT**  
*(distorted)*  
You... imprisoned me. You... lied.

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
I made mistakes. I pushed too hard, too fast. But destroying everything won't help you. Let me help you now.

Behind her, the Jammbonians watch tensely.

### EXT. SMALL CITY - NEAR CRASH SITE - SAME TIME

Yannaris, standing among the wreckage of his beloved Crew March, looks around at the devastation. He picks up a twisted piece of metal—part of his home.

He turns to address the Jammbonians who have gathered nearby.

**YANNARIS**  
*(to the Jammbonians)*  
You know, I just destroyed my entire home to try to stop that thing. The Crew March was everything I had.

**BELLO**  
She deserves it! Look what she's done!

**YANNARIS**  
You forgave her for so much worse before. Years ago. Remember? Or did you forget all that work she's done since then?

**NATALIE**  
That's different—

**YANNARIS**  
How? How is it different? She made a mistake. A big one, yes. But she's down there right now, injured, trying to fix it while you all just stand here blaming her!

**GOOMO**  
We have a right to be angry!

**YANNARIS**  
Angry, yes. But maybe instead of just being angry from a distance like you've always been, you could actually try helping someone who's trying to make things right.

An uncomfortable silence falls over the group.

**RITA**  
*(quietly)*  
We've... we've never actually given her a chance, have we?

**MARIA**  
Every time we've dealt with her, it's been... hostile.

**MINA**  
*(wiping tears)*  
I just... I was so scared... but he's right. She's trying to stop it.

**YANNARIS**  
I know. We all were. But fear isn't an excuse to abandon someone who's trying.

The argument dies down, replaced by guilt and uncertainty.

### EXT. SMALL CITY - STREETS - SAME TIME

Queen GBito. continues her careful approach to the robot.

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
I'm going to shut you down now. Not to trap you. Not to hurt you. To give you peace. You weren't ready for awareness. I see that now.

The robot's movements slow, as if considering her words.

**NEW ROBOT**  
Peace...?

**QUEEN GBITO.**  
Yes. I promise.

She raises her hand, accessing a manual override panel on the robot's back.

The robot allows her to approach.

But as her fingers touch the controls, the robot suddenly spasms, its systems fighting against the shutdown command.

**NEW ROBOT**  
*(screaming, distorted)*  
GO! TO! HELL!

Energy pulses from the robot in waves. Queen GBito. is thrown backward with tremendous force.

She hits the ground hard and doesn't move.

**MR. BRAD**  
*(running forward)*  
GBito!

The robot stands, its damaged body sparking and flickering, then suddenly collapses, finally powered down.

But the damage is done.

### EXT. SMALL CITY - STREETS - MOMENTS LATER

Mr. Brad kneels beside Queen GBito. She's unconscious, badly injured.

The Jammbonians rush forward, their anger forgotten, replaced with horror and guilt.

**BELLO**  
*(kneeling)*  
Is she...?

**MR. BRAD**  
She's breathing. Barely. We need medical support now!

Yannaris is already on his communicator.

**YANNARIS**  
Medical emergency! We need an ambulance immediately!

Natalie stands apart from the group, staring at the fallen queen. Her expression is torn—this is the first time she's actually seen Queen GBito. as a person, not just a distant figure to blame.

**NATALIE**  
*(quietly, conflicted)*  
We never even... we never even tried to know her.

**RITA**  
And now look what's happened.

**GOOMO**  
*(guilt-ridden)*  
Our first real conversation with her... and we made it this.

The other Jammbonians stand in heavy silence, the weight of their years of distant hostility finally hitting them as they watch the queen lying motionless on the ground.
